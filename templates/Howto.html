Changing your Steam Custom URL and Game Details Privacy Via the Website/App

  1. Head to the Steam website/app and log in.
  2. Click on your display name on the top-right of the screen and then select "View Profile" from the dropdown menu.
  3. On the right-hand side of the screen, underneath your level, click "Edit Profile".
  4. In the Custom URL field, type out your desired URL.
  5. Scroll down to the bottom of the page, and click "Save".
  6. On the left side of your screen, click on "Privacy Settings" over the Steam Points Shop. Under "Privacy Settings", "Game Details" and "My profile" should be set to "Public"

Changing your Steam Custom URL and Game Details Privacy on your Phone

  1. Click on your profile picture on the top-right of the screen and then select "View Profile" under your display name.
  2. On top of your screen, click "View Profile" button.
  3. On top of your screen, click "Edit Profile" button.
  4. In the Custom URL field, type in your desired URL.
  5. Scroll down to the bottom of your page and tap "Save".
  6. Scroll up again, then on the scroll menu under your display name, scroll all the way to the right and click on "Privacy Settings".
  7. Under "Privacy Settings", "Game Details" and "My profile" should be set to "Public"
